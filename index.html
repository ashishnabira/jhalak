What is Linux ?

It's an Operating System like Windows built by community contribution

Linux is actually everywhere. It's in your phones, in your cars, in your refrigerators, your modems devices. It runs most of the Internet, the supercomputers making weather forecasts

Linux is worlds biggest collaboration project worked on by lakhs of programmer from 100s of countries over Internet.

Ex: Fedora, Ubuntu, CentOS etc

Why Schools should adopt Linux ?

Its free of cost !!!

Students can learn computing and explore 1000s of opensource softwares

No Viruses at all !!!

They can contribute to opensource softwares

Writing code, Documentation, finding bugs, fixing bugs, creating awareness, etc

How Linux Started ?

Linus Torvalds wrote Linux code and posted on internet in 1991 and requested his friends to use and contribute to it.
